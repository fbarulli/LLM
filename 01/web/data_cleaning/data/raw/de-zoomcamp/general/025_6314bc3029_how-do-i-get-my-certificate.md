There will be two announcements in the course Telegram and Slack channels:

1. To verify your name is correct on the certificate (edit it in your course profile under "Edit Course Profile" if not).
2. When grading is complete and the certificate is ready.

After the second announcement, log into your enrollment page on the cohort's course site (`https://courses.datatalks.club/de-zoomcamp-<year>/enrollment` — the current cohort's URL is in the [course repo](https://github.com/DataTalksClub/data-engineering-zoomcamp)) and follow the instructions in [certificates.md](https://github.com/DataTalksClub/data-engineering-zoomcamp/blob/main/certificates.md) to generate the certificate document.
